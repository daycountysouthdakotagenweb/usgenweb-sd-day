Our Savior’s Lutheran Church

Our Savior’s Lutheran Church was organized December 10, 1910 in the home of Mons Kittleson. It was originally named The Norwegian Lutheran Church.

The first board members: Andrew Hedman as Secretary.

Anton Carlson Mrs. Anton Carlson Elias Johns

Peder Hedman Mrs. Mons Kittles Ole Leines

Einar Nelson Robert Nelson Sina Nelson

 

Ladies Aid was organized around 1908 with Mrs. Anton Carlson as president and Mrs. Louise Erickson as secretary.
