Frank G. Johnson Family

Frank G. Johnson:
Born 23 August 1873 in East Union, Carver County, MN
Died 26 September 1917 in Lily, Day County, SD

Hilda Jonsson:
born 25 September 1875 in Risekatslosa Parish, Skane, Sweden
died 7 December 1938 in Yankton, SD

Frank and Hilda were married on 23 December 1897 in Watertown, SD

Their children, all born and raised in Lily, were:

Irene Johnson Sommers (1899-1987)
Effie Johnson Rice (1901-1987)
Hazel Johnson Jackson (1904- )
Raymond Johnson (1905-1987)
Orlo Johnson (1907-1990)
Leslie "Brick" Johnson (1910-1990)
