PHOTOGRAPHIC HISTORY OF DAY COUNTY


The photographs which follow are the beginning of what we hope will be a large collection of old pictures which describe the early days of Day County. If you have photographs you would like to include in this section, you can submit them in either two ways. This first and perferable method is to scan them to a JPEG file and E-MAIL to me as an attachment. A second method is to send me by "snail mail" a copy (repeat copy) of the photograph. I will do my best to scan them into a file and return them to you.

This is an opportunity to distribute historic Day County photographs to a large audience. So send them in today.
