EBENEZER CEMETERY INDEX
Butler, SD Founded 1892

These pages record burials at Ebenezer Cemetery, Butler, SD, from its beginning. The records have been copied from
information found in a book that has been kept up to date through the years.
Some deaths in the early years do not have the exact
death dates and only the death year is presented.
As far as we know, the data is correct and reliable
Deloris Whitmyre
23 August 2002
