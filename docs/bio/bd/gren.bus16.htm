GRENVILLE

 

Pop 150. Day county. A village on the F & V Ry 18 miles n e of Webster the county seat. Has a Polish Catholic church, a bank, a hotel, a feed mill, and 3 grain elevators. Exp Western. Tel N A. Alb W Lehman P M.
