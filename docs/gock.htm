PHOTOGRAPHIC HISTORY OF DAY COUNTY:
Gockowski Family


The following photographs, submitted by M. Ryan Madison of Coon Rapids, MN, contain images of the Gockowski family who were early residents of Day County, SD. Little information was given concerning the persons photographed. We solicit old pictures that describe the early days and residents of Day County.
