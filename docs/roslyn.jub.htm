ROSLYN DIAMOND JUBILEE
1914-1989

The following alphabetical links are to an index of surnames obtained from pages 75-202 of the Roslyn, South Dakota Diamond Jubilee, 1914-1989 as published by The Reporter & Farmer, Webster, SD, 1989, Library of Congress Catalog Card No. 89-60648.

Care was made to assure accuracy but no warranties are offered. The data is presented here for genealogical purposes in hopes of helping people locate their ancestors. Information transcribed by Karen Kambestad.
