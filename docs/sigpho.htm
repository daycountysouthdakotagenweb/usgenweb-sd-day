SAKARIAS JOHNSON SIGDESTAD Family









Sakarias Jonson Sigdestad immigrated in 1881 to the US with his wife Synneve R. Hjelle and six children. Sakarias(1819-1889) died only 8 years after bringing his family to South Dakota.








This photograph shows (front row, left to right) Mrs. John (Kari) Grove (1854-?), John S. Sigdestad (1865-1950), Synneve (Hjelle) Sigdestad (1831-1919), Rasmus S. Sigdestad (1868-?), (back row) Mrs. Iver (Ingeborg) Skaare (1856-1943), Sakris S. Sigdestad (1874-1953) and Mrs. Magnus (Kristiana) Olson (23 Sep 1861-27 Apr 1927).
