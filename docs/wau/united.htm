United Church of Christ

The first Congregational Church of Waubay was organized February 11, 894 with eighteen charter members. Rev. D. R. Tomlin administered baptism that morning.

Ordination services held in the church for: Rev. Allen Eddy, December 15, 1911 and Rev. Margie Bertsch, July 30, 1978.

The minister at time of print, was Rev. Paul Douglas who also is minister for Summit Methodist Church.
