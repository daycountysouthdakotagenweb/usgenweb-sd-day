Indian Church, Enemy Swim Lake

Indian Church on a hill overlooking the west edge of Enemy Swim Lake the Episcopalians built the church in 1885. A new building was erected in 1922 on the same location. At this time the old church building was move to the base of the hill and served as the Parish Hall until a tragic fire in 1966.
