BUTLER

 

Pop 200. Day County. A village on the C M & St P Ry 14 miles s w of Webster the county seat. Has Methodist, German Evangelical and German Lutheran churches, 2 banks, a hotel and 3 grain elevators. Exp W F Co. Tel W U. H C Sherin P M.
