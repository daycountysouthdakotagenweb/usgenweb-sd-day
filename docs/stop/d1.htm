4. Josephine STOPPEL was born on 10 Sep 1852 in Cincinnati, OH. She died on 12 Dec 1942. She has reference number 1.1.3. She was buried in Minneapolis. Josephina (born September 10, 1852) married John Louis Novack on January 11, 1893, at Brownton. John Novack was a miller by trade and they lived in Brownton and later moved to Minneapolis. Josephina died on December 12, 1942, at the age of 91. John died about 30 years before Josephina. They had one child, Frank.
