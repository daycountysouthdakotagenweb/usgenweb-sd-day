THIRD GENERATION

28. Roy Robert STOPPEL was born on 14 Sep 1895. He died on 19 Jan 1955. He served in the military 8 Jun 1918 to 11 Sep 1919 in Germany: Ammunition truck driver. He has reference number 1.1.5.4.
Roy Robert was born on September 14, 1895. He was an ammunition truck driver in Germany during WWI from June 8, 1918, to September 11, 1919. He married Martha Pauline Schwanke (born September 19, 1898) on October 20, 1920. They had two children: Robert and Paul. Roy died on January 19, 1955, at the age of 59 after being in a coma for two days. He had had surgery on August 31, 1954, when they found he had cancer of the bones and had his right leg amputated on September 2. Martha died on May 24, 1977.

Robert Reinhart was born on June(April?) 26, 1923. He married Eleanor Kirkland on November 29, 1944. He was killed on February 7, 1950, in Riceville, Iowa, when a train hit his truck. He was 26 years old.

Paul Charles was born on May 29, 1925. He graduated from Rochester
High School. He served in the U.S. Army during WWII and was
discharged in 1946. He married Ora Flury on March 22, 1947. He was a farmer, substitute mail carrier, and part-time employee at the Starlite
Outdoor Theater. They had eight children. Paul died on April 10, 1971,
of cancer at the age of 45.

He was married to Martha Pauline SCHWANKE (daughter of Frederick William SCHWANKE and Leontine Pauline RIEGE) on 20 Oct 1920. Martha Pauline SCHWANKE was born on 19 Sep 1898. She died on 24 May 1977. She had Social Security Number 469-42-1037.
