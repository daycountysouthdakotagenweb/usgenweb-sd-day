THIRD GENERATION

35. Erwin Franklin STOPPEL was born on 2 Nov 1898 in Olmsted Co, MN.(9) Soc Sec has his birth date as 2 Nov 1897. He died on 12 Aug 1981 in New Ulm, Brown, MN. He was buried on 17 Aug 1981 in Oakwood Cemetery, Sect 15. He has reference number 1.1.7.1. He had Social Security Number 475-36-6582. Erwin was involved in the family's ownership of real estate and the City Delivery in 1954, he joined his brothers in Stoppel Feed and Seed, until his retirement in 1972.

He was married to Luella JOHANNS on 30 Apr 1924. Married in Simpson, MN. Luella JOHANNS was born on 17 Apr 1901. She died on 6 Dec 1997 in Adel, IA.(12) She had Social Security Number 472-76-0503. Erwin Franklin STOPPEL and Luella JOHANNS had the following children:

+77 i. Duane Erwin STOPPEL.
+78 ii. Donald Francis STOPPEL DVM.
+79 iii. Marilyn Salome STOPPEL.
