8. Frank STOPPEL was born on 28 Mar 1861 in Rochester, MN. He died on 3 Jun 1948 in Olmstd Co. MN. He was buried on 7 Jun 1948 in Oakwood Cemetery, Rochester, MN, Sec 3 Lot 113. Lot 113 Section 3 Oakwood Cemetery He has reference number 1.1.7. Frank farmed and was involved in twonship politics. In 1903 he and his wife moved to Rochester. Frank then sold real estate for a liviing.
