Women in Day County South Dakota

As a source of tracing women, honoring the cooks of the past, and to determine food styles, the following table is an index of surnames for Bergen Lutheran Church, American Lutheran Church Women’s 1973 Cookbook, published by General Publishing and Binding, Iowa Falls, IA.

Care was made to assure accuracy but no warranties are offered. The data is presented here for genealogical purposes in hopes of helping people locate their ancestors. Information transcribed by Karen Kambestad.
