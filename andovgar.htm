LIST OF G. A. R. POSTS IN S. DAK.

 

Alphabetical list of Posts, South Dakota Grand Army of the Republic, with names of veterans residing for a longer or shorter time in

the state.

 

 

ANDOVER

Col. Kirk Post No. 67

 

        This post was mustered July 12, 1884, by A. A. Rowley; had 18 members in 1890, and surrendered its charter in 1900, having eight members.
