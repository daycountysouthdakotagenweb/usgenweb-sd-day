Aaron William Dilley

  Aaron Wiliam Dilley was the son of James William Dilley and Mary Jane La Duke. He was born 21 April 1891 in Waubay, Day Co., South Dakota. Birth: South Dakota State File # 680327. His first wife was named Edith and they had four children; Phyllis (maried M.M. Lory); Margaret (married Earl Barrios); Norman P. and Kenneth A.; Nine grandchildren are mentioned in his will; Terrence N., Kerry M., Warren K., Karis Ann, Norma Jean, Barbara Jo, Jaqueline, Margery and Jeffrey.

Aaron's second Wife was Tillie (Oleson) Carpenter, married 6 August 1959 (my grandmother)

Aaron died 5 Dec 1964 in Huron, Beadle Co., South Dakota and is buried in the Webster Cemetery. His residence at the time of death was 557 Third St SW, Huron, SD
