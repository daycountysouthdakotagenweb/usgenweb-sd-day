THIRD GENERATION

25. Alvin Lenard ASH (1)(2) was born on 15 Jul 1897 in Morocco, Indiana-Newton Co.. He resided in 1939 in Roy, New Mexico. He resided in 1968 in Carlsbad, New Mexico. He died on 4 Dec 1968 in Carlsbad, New Mexico, Eddy Co.. A. L. Ash

Funeral services for A. L. Ash of 1306 W. Bonbright St. will be held Thursday at 3:30 p.m. at Carpenter Denton Chapel with Dr. J. H. L. McAllister of the United Methodist church officiating. He died in a local hospital today.
Ash was born in Indiana. He married Minerva Gay in 1934 at Boise City, Oklahoma , (Cimmaron Co.) They moved to Carlsbad in 1938 where Ash helped with the concrete work in the first power plant for the potash industry in Carlsbad.
He was a retired concrete finisher. Ash was a member of the United Methodist church and Men's Fellowship of United Methodist church.
He is survived by his widow Minerva Ash of Carlsbad and one son, Mark Ash of Carlsbad.


Carlsbad Current-Argus, Wednesday, December 4, 1968
