14. Candace Lenoi ASH (1)(2) was born on 14 Nov 1869 in Morocco, Indiana. She died on 2 Jul 1927 in Sentinel Butte, North Dakota. She was born in May be 1870. She was buried in Prairie Mound Cemetery, Butler SD. More Wedding Bells
W. A. Hart of Rolla, North Dakota, was in Minneapolis last week, and so was Miss Candace Lenoi Ash of this place. Miss Maud Ash too is in Minneapolis and Mr. and Mrs. McCloskey of Garden City were there.
Out of these facts were shaped an item of news that was a great surprise to Butler and vicinity. Mr. Hart and Miss Ash were united in marriage on Monday, July 23, and announced that they will be at home in Rolla, North Dakota after September 15th. They are now spending their honeymoon in Duluthll, Minnesota.
Mr. Hart is an old resident of Butler and Miss Ash has spent most of her life there. Both are well liked and have the best wishes ofj a host of friends. We will try to forgive them for going off without a word of warning. We like to be economical but it is a pleasure to be lavish with rice and charivari music.


A Daughter At Hart's
A tiny maiden came on Thursday, February 18, to cheer the home and brighten the lives of Mr. and Mrs. W.A. Hart, of this city. The mother is getting along nicely and Mr. Hart is perfectly able-and willing- to buy and present to his friends the best cigars in town-in fact he seems highly pleased about it. The little girl's name is Genevieve Laurinda Hart-which will doubtless be sufficient for her needs for twenty years or thereabouts.
